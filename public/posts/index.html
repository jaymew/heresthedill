Bicycling is one of my favorite things about living in Germany. There are clearly marked lanes specifically for bicyclists and should you accidently wander into one you will be reminded by angry shouts and ringing bells or- simply run over. Motorists, the majority of the time, are fairly respectful of bikes at least in the city I live in. But there is one major downside when you don’t have a car and that is space and weight.

If you are as nerdy and passionate about gardening as I am, welcome! I’ve always worked in a garden and I’ve constantly been curious to the why behind many processes in the garden. Plants and their interactions within the world have constantly fascinated me and this website is an exploration to those nagging questions that we all come across while gardening. I really want to gain a deeper understanding of the biological, ecological and chemical mechanisms occurring in our gardens, as well as agricultural and environmental topics surrounding food in order to become a better gardener and a more conscious citizen of the world. Successes and failures will be shared along with any research behind them.
